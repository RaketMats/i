# The Design Conversation
It is necessary to establish a conversation around design work. It is very easy to just start working on problems and even more easy to start working on the solution.  
It is important to discuss the problems, discuss different solutions. What need to be done. How shall we work.
- Workshops shall not be done without an agenda.
- Working on a problem and solutions shall not be done untill a conversation on what to prioritize. How work shall be done. What we expect to learn.

# A Structured Approach
It is important to design digital products using a structured approach:
- using architectural frameworks, since we address digital products.
- using different framings, such as users, business, organisation

Digital products are very complex. To address all aspects some kind of map of what a typical solution or product is, is needed. To be kept as a reference, to have in the corner of the eye.

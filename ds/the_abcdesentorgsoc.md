# About
This is an architectural and design pattern with many depths.  
- It is preferrably done in Edgy.  
- It represents a simple concept of use cases and functionality that solves these.
- It represents the business model through reconciliation of use cases(income) and functions(cost)
- Functions are both the architectural form, capabilities, activities and information and architectural structure which is the implementation. 
  - Here the object oriented approach with abstract base classes maps directly to the ABCDEsEnt model.
- It also represents organisation, touchpoints, business idea and other aspects.

# The Importance of Design Management

It is important that there exist a clear design management way of working. The book 'Design Management'(Österlin) describes this well. In short, again, it is important that the Team owns the design process since this is fundamentally different from project management, and that there is a clear line and clear responsibilites and most important, practical organisation such as a place, people, mandate realised.  
To start listing some good tools:
- The book 'Enterprise Design Patterns' both contains good examples on design management but also, introduces the concept of 'patterns' which has a long history within software development.
